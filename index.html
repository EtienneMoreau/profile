My favorite mountain movies

Meru

After suffering dramatic set backs in their lives, three close friends who are among the world's best professional climbers battle their complicated pasts, inner demons and nature's harshest elements in an attempt to confront the Shark's Fin on Mount Meru.

A Fine Line

A Fine Line takes us on a breathtaking journey over the spectacular snow-covered peaks of the Alps as we join a small group of extraordinary mountain athletes in their pursuit of happiness and fulfillment.

Messner, Profession Alpiniste

Né en 1944 dans le Haut-Adige, Reinhold Messner a été entraîné dès son enfance par son père à gravir les sommets. Il a depuis escaladé les quatorze montagnes du monde culminant à plus de 8000 mètres.
